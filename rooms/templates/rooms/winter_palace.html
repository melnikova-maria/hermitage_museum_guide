SOON Fedor Yakovlevich Alekseev

Fedor Alexeev’s painting “View of Moscow. Educational Home” depicts the educational building located on the banks of the river in the late XVIII - early XIX centuries.

In the picture, the author masterfully shows the architecture of the building, the trees and the landscape around it, emphasizing the unity of the man and nature.

SOON Balthasar van der Ast

Balthasar van der Ast is a Dutch painter who specialized in still life paintings. His work is characterized by vibrant colors, detailed brushwork, and an emphasis on realism.

One of his most famous works is "Still life with Fruits", which features a variety of fruit arranged on a tabletop in a realistic and naturalistic manner. This painting captures the freshness and and is an example of van der Ast's mastery of still life painting.

SOON Hubert Robert

Hubert Robert is a French landscape painter known for his beautiful and serene paintings of rural landscapes. He is considered one of the most important landscape painters of the 19th century.

His "Architectural Landscape with Canal" is one of his most famous pieces, featuring a calm and peaceful scene with buildings, boats, and trees, bathed in warm sunlight. The painting is characterized by muted colors and refined brushwork, creating a sense of depth and motion in the scene.

SOON Jacob Philipp Heckert

Jacob Philipp Heckert is a German landscape painter who is known for his lush and naturalistic landscapes.

His painting The Patron's Villa and Waterfalls in Tivoli depicts a serene and peaceful setting in Italy, surrounded by waterfalls and lush trees. The colors used are vibrant and rich with greens, blues and yellows. The brushwork used is smooth and fluid which creates the illusion of motion in the water and the trees.

SOON Luis Melendez

Luis Melendez is a Spanish painter known for creating vibrant and colorful still-life paintings.

In "Still-Life with Figs" he captures the essence of Spanish Golden Age artistry through bright jewel-like hues and intricate brushstrokes. The figs arranged realistically on the table capture the freshness of the moment, giving this piece a sense of vibrancy and movement.

PORTRAIT OF AN OLD WOMAN

The painting "Portrait of an Old Lady" by Bassano Leandro is a masterpiece that captures the essence of age and wisdom. It depicts a woman who is sitting on a chair in front of a table. She is wearing a bright red dress with a dark blue cape. Her face is covered in wrinkles.

BATTLE OF MOSCOW SEPTEMBER 7, 1812

“The Battle of Moscow,” by Adam and Albrécht, portrays a pivotal moment in the Russian-French War of 1812 when the Russian Army fought off Napoleon’s troops. The painting depicts the decisive three-day battle that took place on 7 September 1812 in which the French suffered heavy losses while the Russians managed to delay the advancing French army.

AUTHOR: Jacques-Louis David
